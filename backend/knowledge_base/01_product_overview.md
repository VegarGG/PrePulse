# PrePulse — Product Overview

PrePulse is a preemptive, agentic-AI cybersecurity platform built for small
and mid-market organizations that cannot afford a dedicated SOC. Instead of
reacting to alerts after an attack, PrePulse identifies threats in the wild,
validates whether the company is actually exposed to them, and stages
defensive actions — all before an attacker lands a payload.

## Core differentiation

PrePulse ships an **agent fleet** that plans, validates, and acts, rather
than a single copilot bolted onto a legacy SIEM. It prices on outcomes
(incidents averted, mean-time-to-contain) rather than per-endpoint
telemetry volume. Every reasoning step is observable through traced
decision chains, and staged autonomy ensures a human approval threshold
sits in front of any potentially destructive action.

## Four pillars

1. **Predictive threat intelligence** — continuously ingest external feeds
   tailored to the customer's industry and tech stack.
2. **Adversarial exposure validation (AEV)** — confirm which exposures are
   actually exploitable for *this* customer, not just listed in a CVE
   database.
3. **Automated moving target defense (AMTD)** — rotate ports, certificates,
   and credentials preemptively to invalidate attacker reconnaissance.
4. **Autonomous agentic security operations** — chain evidence, draft
   containment plans, and execute approved playbooks with rollback.

## Target customer

Companies in the 30–500 employee band, especially in **fintech**,
**healthcare**, and **e-commerce**, that hold sensitive data but cannot
staff a 24/7 security operations center.
